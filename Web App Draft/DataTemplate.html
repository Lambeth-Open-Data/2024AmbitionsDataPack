Making Lambeth Neighbourhoods Fit for the Future

Introduction

By 2030, Lambeth aims to be a clean, vibrant, and climate-resilient borough, with residents committed to healthier and happier lives. This requires a fundamental shift in mindset and action, advocating for national environmental changes while locally reimagining transportation, architecture, and lifestyle choices for a sustainable future.
